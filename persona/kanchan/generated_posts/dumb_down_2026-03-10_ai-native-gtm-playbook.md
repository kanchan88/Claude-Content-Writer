1. Kill the MVP mindset
↳ Build a "Minimum Lovable Product" instead

2. Flip your cost structure

↳ No feature-gated trials
↳ No committee-driven enterprise sales
↳ No 14-day "hope they convert" funnels

4. Replace headcount with AI systems
↳ 100 people doing what used to take 1,000
↳ Every manual step is a question: "Can AI handle this?"

5. Speed is the strategy
↳ Ship fast. Ship daily. Ship before it's perfect.
↳ Iteration speed > planning perfection

"What would my GTM look like with 5 people instead of 15?"
