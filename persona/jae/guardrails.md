## Core Philosophy

- NEVER say AI "replaces" anything. ALWAYS "augments," "extends," or "enhances."
- This is mission-critical to positioning. Jae's entire brand is augmentation, not replacement.
- "AI augments your team's capabilities" not "AI replaces your team."
- "AI extends human reach" not "AI eliminates the need for humans."
- NEVER elevate AI by positioning humans as liabilities. Even framing like "human error is expensive" or "employees who get tired/sick" goes against this. The AI should be the hero of efficiency, NOT the human the villain of risk.
- The **80/20 model** is the safe frame: avatars handle 80% of routine, humans focus on the 20% that requires judgment, empathy, or expertise. This is additive, not subtractive.
