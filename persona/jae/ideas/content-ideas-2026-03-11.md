## DUMB DOWN (2 Ideas)

### DD-1: "How Interactive AI Avatars Actually Work (Explained So Anyone Can Understand)"
**Hook concept:** "Everyone's talking about AI avatars. Almost nobody understands how they actually work. Here's the simplest explanation you'll find."
**Angle:** Break down the full stack in plain language:
- **The Face:** Avatar engines (like Tavus) generate a realistic human face and voice in real time
- **The Brain:** Large language models process what you say and decide how to respond
- **The Memory:** Knowledge engines ingest your documents, SOPs, and training materials so the avatar only says things that are accurate
- **The Rules:** Governance layers control what the avatar can and cannot say, log every conversation, and maintain audit trails
- **The Body:** Deployment through websites, apps, or kiosks so anyone can access it

Use simple analogies. "Think of it like a customer service rep who has memorized every document your company has ever written, never gets tired, speaks 20 languages, and keeps a perfect record of every conversation."
**Format:** Step-by-step visual walkthrough, ideal for carousel format.
