## Design Patterns Summary (What Wins on LinkedIn)

### Top 5 Image Archetypes by Engagement

| Rank | Archetype | Avg Comments | Examples |
|------|-----------|-------------|----------|
| 1 | **Tool Pairing / Split Screen** | 3,500+ | Images 2, 3 |
| 2 | **Product Preview / Landing Card** | 3,000+ | Images 5, 9 |

| 4 | **Dark System Architecture** | 800+ | Images 7, 10, 12 |
| 5 | **Minimal Brand Hero** | 500+ | Images 1, 8, 11 |

### Color Themes

| Theme | Usage | When to Use |
|-------|-------|-------------|
| **Dark mode** (navy/black) | 50% of images | Technical/system content, premium feel, architecture diagrams |
| **Light/white** | 33% of images | Tool pairings, document previews, minimal hero cards |
| **Warm accent** (terracotta/salmon) | 17% of images | Product launches, landing cards, approachable feel |

### Typography Patterns
- **Headers:** Bold sans-serif or condensed sans-serif, often ALL CAPS with letter-spacing
- **Body:** Clean sans-serif, medium weight, high contrast against background
- **Accent text:** Monospace/terminal font for technical credibility
- **Highlight technique:** Colored background boxes behind key numbers/stats

### Layout Rules
- **Square (1:1):** Most versatile, works for dashboards, product cards, tool pairings
- **Portrait (4:5):** Best for infographics, flow charts, document previews (more feed real estate)
- **Landscape (16:9):** Best for simple tool pairings, minimal hero cards

### Recurring Design Elements
1. **Brand logos as visual shorthand** (Claude asterisk, Figma, LinkedIn logo) - instant recognition
2. **"+" connector** between tool logos - communicates integration
3. **Glass-morphism cards** on dark backgrounds - premium SaaS feel
4. **Pixelated/mosaic borders** - distinctive visual signature (Ira Bodnar's brand)
5. **Blurred background content** - creates FOMO, teases value behind the download
6. **Color-coded numbering** - makes lists scannable and visually engaging
7. **Subtle corner/edge decorations** - dotted borders, accent shapes
8. **Creator avatar + brand** in footer or header - consistent personal branding
9. **CTA baked into the image** - "Comment [KEYWORD]" button/text in the visual itself
10. **Monospace/terminal fonts** - signals technical depth and credibility
