# LinkedIn Lead Magnet Strategy

---

## Core Concept

A lead magnet is a **high-value document** given away free on LinkedIn in exchange for a comment or email opt-in. The document itself is always tied to a **timely angle** — a trend, news event, or fresh perspective relevant to your ICP — making it feel urgent and worth having.

The goal isn't downloads. It's **starting conversations with pre-qualified prospects** who self-select by engaging.

---

## Two Deployment Formats

| Format | Mechanic | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| **Comment-for** | "Comment X and I'll send it" | Maximizing reach & engagement |
| **Link-to-landing page** | Direct link in post | Higher-intent, quieter ICPs |

Comment-for drives more algorithm visibility. Link-to drives cleaner opt-in data. Use both across your content calendar.

---

## The Document Itself

**What to create:**
- High-level, strategic content (not tactical step-by-steps)
- Tied to a current trend, news angle, or emerging problem your ICP faces
- Something that feels like insider knowledge — not generic advice

**Perceived value levers:**
- Reference time invested ("took 40 hours to build")
- Position it as something only paid clients receive
- Include bonuses stacked on top of the core resource
- Fulfill on the promise — strong free content signals stronger paid content

---

## Post Structure (Comment-for Format)

1. **Hook** — Outcome + timeframe + mechanism. Add "I'm giving this away free."
2. **Outline** — 5–6 bullet points of what's inside
3. **Value stack** — Bonuses, FOMO triggers, exclusivity framing
4. **CTA** — Comment to receive + ask them to connect (connections = followers)
5. **Media** — Reiterate the hook visually + show proof of the outcome (calendar, revenue screenshot, etc.)

---

## Lead Flow After the Post

```
Post goes live
    ↓
Comments / clicks accumulate
    ↓
Leads opt-in via landing page (name + email ± phone)
    ↓
Enter CRM / email sequence
    ↓
Nurture → book a call → close
```

Filter commenters through Sales Navigator to identify only ICP-matching leads before outreach. Don't message everyone — message the right people.

---

## Content Cadence

- Post **daily** (weekdays + weekends)
- Mix: lead magnets + social proof posts + soft CTA posts
- **Repurpose** lead magnets — the same asset can be re-posted with a new angle or hook
- Cross-post reformatted versions to Twitter/X with a single link in bio

---

## Key Strategic Principle

Free content quality = perceived value of paid offer.

Give away your best thinking. The lead magnet is not the product — it's the proof that you know what you're talking about.
